Summary — March 2026
ShopBack NZ
Geo PM: 11.3% blended (goal 35%+) · Went negative week of Mar 22-28 (-4.32% PM)
NZ margin was largely healthy through the first three weeks of March, then went negative in week 4 and worsened further in week 5. Volume is not the issue — OWL loads held flat at 18-20K every single week with no dip. The margin flip is entirely driven by two campaign-level pricing problems converging in the same week: Solitaire iOS/Android have been bleeding since week 2 with payout accelerating while revenue declines (event calibration issue), and Cryptogram iOS/Android launched Mar 15, ran healthy for one week, then immediately flipped negative. Combined campaign losses: -$5.5K across 5 flagged campaigns.
Volume
Not the issue. OWL loads flat 18-20K all month. Traffic is healthy.
Root cause
Campaign pricing. Solitaire event calibration off since wk 2. Cryptogram miscalibrated at launch.
Urgency
High. Solitaire has been worsening for 4 weeks. Cryptogram added a second bleed layer.
AM action
Review event calibration on Solitaire. Review launch payout rates on Cryptogram.
ShopBack MY
Geo PM: 7.8% blended (goal 35%+) · Negative or near-zero 3 of 5 weeks
MY is a more complex story with two separate issues — campaign pricing and a real volume decline. OWL loads dropped 23% across March (111K week 1 to 86K week 4) and clicks fell from 17.8K to 10.7K. However the margin was already negative in week 2 when volume was still at 100K loads, so the campaign issues were the primary driver. BlossomMatch Android and iOS launched Mar 1 with payout rates set too high from day 1 — negative all month. CM Android (29517) was paused after week 1 but paid out delayed conversions for 4 more weeks against $0 revenue. Combined campaign losses: -$6.8K across 4 flagged campaigns.
Volume
Declining — -23% across March. Contributing factor but not the primary driver.
Root cause
Campaign pricing + delayed payouts. BlossomMatch miscalibrated at launch. CM Android payout tail post-pause.
Urgency
High. BlossomMatch has been negative since Mar 1. Structural, not a blip.
AM action
Review BlossomMatch payout rates. Investigate volume decline — separate issue from campaign pricing.

How to update this dashboard
1
Pull the app stats export from Metabase for the relevant geos, grouped by week for the month. This gives OWL loads, clicks, conversions, revenue, payout, PM% — the volume and margin trends.
2
Pull the campaign-level historical report for each flagged geo, grouped by week. This surfaces which specific campaigns are driving the margin issues.
3
Upload both CSVs to Claude and specify the geo. Claude identifies flagged campaigns, root causes, and updates the data block in this file.
4
Upload the new HTML to GitHub. Same link, updated content. Share with AMs and reference from the margin dashboard.
